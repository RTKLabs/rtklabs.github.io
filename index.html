Game Development Crash Course

What is it?

We teach game development by taking your child from making their own custom graphics (sprites) all the way to coding their own full featured game!


How is it executed?

Our crash course is broken up into two parts. In the first part, your child will use learn how to use Piskel, an online editor specifically designed for creating pixel art. Once your child has comfortably grasped the basics of pixel art with Piskel, we move to the 2nd part of the course: coding! In the 2nd part of our course, we have your child create their first game using the pixels they made and build a game using the Scratch visual programming language. In other words, your child builds entire game from the ground up!


How long is it?

The course runs for a few hours at most. Think about 6 - 7 hours.


What else do I need to know?

Not much! An imagination and overall curiousity will help! No software to install as both Scratch and Piskel are available online!
