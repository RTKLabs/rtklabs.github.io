OurStory

We know what it's like to stare at a computer screen in wonder.
Yes, we have been there and we're now on the other side (literally and figuratively of course).
We're not staring anymore. Ok, maybe not as much since we work behind scenes to create
some of the cool stuff you see before your eyes. That's our goal first and foremost at RTK Labs,
helping you get started in becoming a coder so you too can make all the cool moving things you
see on screen too!

RTK Labs came about with the idea that coding is a skill and learning it shouldn't be hard.
We're an organization of learners, tech enthusiasts, and just overall curious people. In
fact, our curious nature was what brought us from seeing stuff on a computer screen once
upon a time to actually make it. RTK Labs aims to make that very experience your own, providing
you the tools and platform to become a full-fledged coder and obtain the most sought-after skill
of the 21st century.
