OurStory

RTK Labs was founded on the belief that knowing how to code is a skill
that everyone should have: young and old. We realized that the problem isn't so much
that coding is a hard skill but one that requires the "right" approach and consistency.
We are a group of industry professionals who once upon time didn't know a variable
from a method. Our experience has taught us that coding isn't some black box or a "hard" skill.
We pass the benefits of our years of experience to you. Join us as we bring you on the journey of
a lifetime, taking you from absolute beginner to a solid coder. Coding doesn't have to be
hard, especially when you have the right teachers.
